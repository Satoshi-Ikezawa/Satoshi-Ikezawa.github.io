About

早稲田大学にて、メタサーフェス・メタレンズによる光波制御、 レーザ計測、ナノフォトニクス、先端光応用に関する研究を行っています。 光と物質の相互作用を基盤に、新しい光学原理と実装技術の創出を目指しています。

Since 2019, I have been developing metasurface and metalens technologies for advanced optical wavefront control, including full-color holography, polarization-splitting varifocal metalenses, and multifunctional integrated metasurfaces.

Research Interests

Metasurfaces and Metalenses

Design, fabrication, and characterization of ultrathin optical elements for wavefront control, focusing, beam shaping, and multifunctional optics.

Optical Wavefront Control

Exploration of new principles for manipulating phase, polarization, propagation direction, and spatial optical fields.

Laser Diagnostics and Optical Sensing

Laser-based measurement, spectroscopy, and optical diagnostics for advanced sensing and light–matter interaction studies.

Quantum and Advanced Optics

Application of metasurface optics to quantum technologies, optical trapping, compact optical systems, and integrated photonics.

Research Highlights

Full-color holography

Metasurface-based holographic projection for full-color optical image generation.

Metasurface Holography

Polarization-splitting varifocal metalens

Integration of polarization separation and tunable focusing functions in an ultrathin metalens platform.

Metalens Varifocal optics

Multifunctional integrated metasurface

Integration of lens, prism, and waveplate functions into a single compact metasurface device.
