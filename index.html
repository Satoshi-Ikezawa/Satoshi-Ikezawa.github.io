池沢 聡 / Satoshi Ikezawa

早稲田大学にて、メタサーフェス・メタレンズによる光波制御、 レーザ計測、ナノフォトニクス、先端光応用に関する研究を行っています。

I am a researcher at Waseda University working on metasurfaces, metalenses, optical wavefront control, laser diagnostics, and nanophotonics.

Research Interests

  • Metasurfaces and metalenses
  • Optical wavefront control
  • Laser diagnostics and optical measurement
  • Nanophotonics and light–matter interaction
  • Advanced optics for quantum technologies and optical sensing

Highlights

  • Full-color holography using metasurfaces
  • Polarization-splitting varifocal metalenses
  • Multifunctional integrated metasurfaces
  • Founder/originator of the Meta-Optics Research Group, Optical Society of Japan
